Emma Fordham is a Swedish/British artist currently living and working in Hong Kong. Since graduating with a degree in painting from the Slade School of Fine Art and a PGCE in Art and Design she has worked, exhibited and taught Art and Textiles in Sweden, England, Shanghai and Hong Kong.  
Working through the mediums of painting, drawing and stitching her work explores themes around landscape, memory, urbanization and the natural world.
